ABOUT US

Hidden Treasure discovery Initiative is a non-profit and non political organization Nrs. Felicia Titilayo Hinmikaiye is the sole owner of the "Hidden Treasure Discovery Initiative"


She started the programme as "Talent Hunt" a pet project in 2002 at Dansol High School, Agidingbi, Ikeja, Lagos.


Many of Dansol High School students have benefitted from the project and so the owner intends making it global so that others can also benefit from it. Under this global outreach, it is called "Hidden Treasure Discovery Initiative."


All the aims and objectives clearly stated, have been achieved while the chairman is all working as a Career and Guidance Counsellor at Dansol High School. Some students that have benefited from the programme are Olele Christopher, Adeleke Dolapo, Dream Starz group, etc.


In view of the present global economic predicament and social vices especially in Africa. This programme will enable the participants to acquire skills that will enable them to take a decision whether to work with their certificates or use their acquired skills after graduation. The programme will also solve the programme will also solve the problemof social vices as every one of them will be angaged. This initiative also hopes to have a vocational center.


The Demo for this project will feature Dansol students who have participated in the Talent Hunt Editions in Dansol High School. The organizer of this programme has been Mrs HinmikaiyeTitilayo Felicia who also works as Career and Guidance Counsellor. Therefore she remains the sole owner of this programme.
